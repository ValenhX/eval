Industries

DEFENSE & SPACE

We Deliver a Mission Advantage

Our electronic and mechanical engineered components are on a wide array of existing and emerging defense and space systems, delivering reliable, high-performance solutions that meet the demands of increasingly integrated and mission-critical environment. Arxis delivers critical, highly specialized components capable of operating reliably under extreme conditions across air, land, sea, and space domains.

COMMERCIAL AEROSPACE

Enabling the Future of Aerospace

Arxis designs and manufactures engineered components that are on a broad set of platforms in the commercial aerospace and business aviation sectors. Our capabilities meet the industry’s most demanding performance, quality, and regulatory requirements. We partner closely with OEMs and Tier 1 suppliers to support production, fleet modernization, and next-generation aircraft programs.

INDUSTRIAL TECHNOLOGY

Highly Engineered Components for the Most Demanding Applications

Arxis is a key provider of engineered components to the Industrial Technology market, delivering vital engineered solutions to the Medical Technology and Specialized Industrials sectors.
